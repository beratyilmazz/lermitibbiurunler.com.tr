Who We Are?

Lermi Tıbbi Ürünler Tic. Ltd. Şti.

LERMI TIBBI URUNLER TİCARET LİMİTED ŞİRKETİ was established to serve in the health sector and started its activities in 2023. Pioneer in the Surgical Instruments market, originating in Pakistan from the producer countries, produced with high quality and 65 years of experience, It is the only authorized representative of QAYUM & ELAHISONS PVT LTD brand in Turkey.

QAYUM & ELAHISONS PVT LTD surgical instruments is the first surgical instrument factory established in Pakistan in 1958 as a family business. High quality production, established in 65 countries It has been continuing for 65 years with its huge sales network.

With the friendship and trust-based business partnership established between QAYUM & ELAHISONS PVT LTD and LERMİ TIBBI URUNLER TİCARET LİMİTED ŞİRKETİ, we are determined to change the prejudices in the Turkish Market, with a high quality and fully guaranteed product range that appeals to all surgical branches and has a Dental portfolio.
